Rafael Lima

Indie Game Developer

Hi! I'm Rafael Lima and I created this website to share my projects and my passion about games. I'm years old and live in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. At the moment I'm studying Information Systems at UNIRIO (Federal University of the State of Rio de Janeiro), and I've been studying game development since 2020.


As far as I can remember, I've been a huge fan of games, from video games to tabletop games. When the Covid-19 pandemic hit my country, I started to play games even more, as an escape valve. From that point, I realized how important games are, and how they can change people lives positively. So I took all the programming knowledge and skills that I developed in college and brought them to this universe of game making, and just fell in love with it. Right now I'm taking a lot of courses, reading books, and grabbing every opportunity that I get to do what I love.


Here you can find a section where I share my skills and another section with my recent projects, mostly done just to study and practice. On top of that, you can also find all my social media and e-mail, where you can contact me.
